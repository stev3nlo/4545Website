Team 4545 Ouroboros

Hello, we are team 4545 Ouroboros, and we are part of the Viperbots FTC Robotics program at Vandegrift High School in Austin, Texas.

Find Out More

Our History

Our team was the first Vandegrift robotics team under the original name, ViperBots, created in 2010. Five years later, with the growth and success of team 4545, ViperBots has expanded into an organization that consists of six different unique teams. In our organization, all the teams try their best to represent ViperBots.

Meet the Team

Ouroboros is a team that promotes gracious professionalism and the betterment of the FIRST community. We have 12 members who also participate in Cross Country, Track, YMSL, Tennis, NCL, Band, NHS, Deca, and Cyber Patriot Team.

We have 7 Hardware, 6 Software, and everyone helps out with marketing. Our team consists of 2 freshmen, 6 Sophomores, 3 Juniors, and 1 Senior. This diversity helps us brainstorm and produce new and innovative ideas.
